About Us

Founded in 1987, our business has grown year by year. We understand the industry and our clients, we enjoy a stable track record - and this sets us apart.

At the beginning, TOP RANK focused on the reliable quality supply of active pharmaceutical ingredients for human and veterinary needs. It was in Chottogram (former Chittagong) in early 1987 we sailed for the journey towards quality ingredients.

As a service provider offering good customer service, TOP RANK responds to the changing needs of its business partners in Bangladesh and abroad. It is necessary, because increasingly strict regulatory and qualitative requirements on the pharmaceutical market require safeguarding of quality and documentation of products. The demand for professional support in this area has also increased over these years and we have built a strong team to take care.
